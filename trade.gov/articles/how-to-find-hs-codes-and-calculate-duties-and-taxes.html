How to Find HS Codes and Calculate Duties and Taxes

May 14, 2013
This webinar features Senior International Trade Specialist Dr. Doug Barry as he informs listeners how to classify their goods for shipment in the Harmonized System which is standard throughout the world through the Census Bureau. Following this, he takes the participants through each resource that the Trade Information Center has to find out the duties and taxes for your shipment, including the Country Specific Tariff and Tax information database as well as Customs information.
