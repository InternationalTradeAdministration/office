Tariff Publication The International Customs Journal, published by the International Customs Tariffs Bureau (BITD), covers duties indexed by HS numbers for individual countries. The timeliness of the tariff schedules varies from country to country (i.e., new schedules are not published in consistent time intervals). Schedule volumes can be purchased from the National Technical Information Service (NTIS) by phone at 1-800-553-6847 or (703) 605-6000.
