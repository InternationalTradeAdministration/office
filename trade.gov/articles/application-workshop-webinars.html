Application Workshop Webinars

March 8, 2013
We will host two webinars for potential applicants or trade specialists to review the application process and answer questions. Registration for the webinars is required. The webinars have been scheduled as follows: “E” Award or “E Star” Award for Exports - Tuesday, November 27, 2012 @3:00 p.m. EST “E” Award or “E Star” Award for Export Service - Thursday, November 29, 2012 @3:00 p.m. EST To access the webinars you will need a computer with internet connection and an additional telephone line.
