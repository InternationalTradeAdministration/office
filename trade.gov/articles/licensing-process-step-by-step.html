No Export License/Authorization Needed After consulting with the appropriate U.S. Government agencies, U.S. companies can at times engage in certain activities related to civil nuclear trade in foreign markets that do not require a license/authorization. Such activities may include: holding meetings with government officials, potential customers and partners; and, participating in trade missions and other similar business development activities, as long as information shared is public and not business-proprietary.
