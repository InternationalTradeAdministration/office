Alestra AT&T Recognized by U.S. Commercial Service

January 11, 2013
A “Certificate of Appreciation for Achievement in Trade" was presented to Daniel Echauri, Marketing Director of the Mexican company Alestra AT&T by the U.S. Commercial Service in Mexico City. Alestra AT&T was honored for sponsoring the American pavilion at Expo Comm México for the last four years. Alestra has also supported U.S. companies with their appointments with the Mexican companies and has provided relevant information on the industry. With its network of offices across the United States and in more than 80 countries, the U.S. Commercial Service of the U.S. Department of Commerce's International Trade Administration utilizes its global presence and international marketing expertise to help U.S. companies sell their products and services worldwide.
