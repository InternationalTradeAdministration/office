Combustion Associates of CA

May 13, 2013
Combustion Associates of Corona, California is a small business created by immigrants from Bangladesh. The company makes large power generators that have found markets in many developing countries.  Combustion Associates recently won the prestigious Department of Commerce E-Award for excellence in exporting.  The video features the husband and wife team of founders sharing the story of how they built the business from nothing to where it now sells product in more than 40 different countries.   Download the full video (8 Mb)
