Arcor Internacional, S.A. Recognized by the U.S. Commercial Service

January 11, 2013
A “Certificate of Appreciation for Achievement in Trade” was presented to Mr. Rodolfo Ortíz, of the Mexican company Arcor Internacional, S.A. by the U.S. Commercial Service in Mexico City. Arcor Internacional became the official representative of the U.S. company Revive Skincare Products and was recognized by the CS for contributing to U.S. exports. With its network of offices across the United States and in more than 80 countries, the U.S. Commercial Service of the U.S. Department of Commerce's International Trade Administration utilizes its global presence and international marketing expertise to help U.S. companies sell their products and services worldwide.
