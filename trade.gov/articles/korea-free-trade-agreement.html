Korea Free Trade Agreement

May 14, 2013
In this webinar, you will learn about the market opportunities in Korea and how to take advantage of the recent free trade agreement with Korea from our main speaker who is from the Foreign Commercial Service at the Department of Commerce. You will learn about opportunities from an international trade and customs law firm perspective and from that of a freight forwarder with experience doing business in the region.
