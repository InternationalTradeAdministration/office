Foreign Trade Regulations’ Filing Requirements

May 13, 2013
This webinar will provide the basics for understanding the reporting requirements found in the Foreign Trade Regulations (FTR). Specialists from the Regulations Outreach and Education Branch of the Census Bureau will discuss the importance of reporting accurate export information. They will explain the legal requirements, the filing timeframes and the responsibilities of the parties involved in a U.S. export transaction. Download the full video (196 Mb)
