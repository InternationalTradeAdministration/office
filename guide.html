Minnesota District Export Council

The Minnesota District Export Council is a private, non-profit organization that brings together experienced international business people who provide guidance and assistance in international markets.

The Minnesota District Export Council encourages and supports:
• Exports that strengthen individual companies, stimulate U.S. economic growth, and create jobs;
• Export expansion activities by working with the US & FCS domestic offices; and
• Opportunities to promote greater export activity at the local level by developing a trade assistance network.
